About me

Front-end developer from Europe/Netherlands

Hey there, lovely people! Allow me to introduce myself—I'm Derya Deniz, your friendly neighborhood coder and art enthusiast.

At the age of 28, I've embarked on an exciting journey to blend my love for coding with my passion for creativity. 🎨🖥️ When I'm not immersed in lines of code, you can find me surrounded by a delightful bunch of 15 cats. 🐱❤️

Art has always held a special place in my heart, and I've had the incredible opportunity to showcase my work in both group and personal exhibitions. In 2018, my art graced the walls of a captivating exhibition in Turkey, and in 2020, I had the honor of sharing my creativity in a personal exhibition in the land of the rising sun, Japan! 🌟✨🎉

As destiny would have it, my husband and I recently embarked on a new adventure in the Netherlands. Although I couldn't continue my teaching journey here, I found solace in coding and decided to take up front-end development. 🚀🌈 Joining SheCodes was a game-changer—I've already earned two certifications and crafted some awesome projects!

While I dabbled in coding before, it's here that I'm truly embracing the world of front-end wizardry. I'm thrilled to learn, grow, and connect with the vibrant tech community! Let's create some magic together! ✨🌟💻
